Diversity of the arsenal

Players use various techniques to trick their opponents and get past them:

  • Dribbling
  • Special skills(feints)

dribbling is maneuvering a ball by one player while moving in a given direction, avoiding defenders' attempts to intercept the ball

feint is a player deceiving the opposition into believing he is going to pass, shoot, move in a certain direction, or receive the ball and instead doing something entirely different, thus gaining an advantage
